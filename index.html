Results

All experiments are run on full resolution and on the 30k iterations for all pipelines. For Our pipeline we use the lambda values λdepth=0.1, λnormals=0.05 for depth and normal loss regularization. Our pipeline runs the intersection depth rendering at the entry point. For the 10 views results we selected 10 views based on maximizing the visible COLMAP points.
